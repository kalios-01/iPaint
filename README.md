
# iPaint for Kids 🎨 

iPaint for Kids is a fun and engaging drawing app designed especially for children. With an easy-to-use interface, kids can explore their creativity, draw, paint, and share their art with friends. The app also provides a seamless option to save drawings to the gallery with just a tap.


## Features

- 🖌️ User-Friendly Interface: Intuitive controls perfect for kids of all ages.
- 🎨 Multiple Drawing Tools: Choose from various brushes, colors,  and shapes to create unique artworks.
- 💾 Save to Gallery: Save drawings directly to the device gallery.
- 📤 Share Creations: Easily share art with friends and family.
- 🚫 Ad-Free & Safe: No ads, providing a safe environment for kids.

## How It Works
- Select your desired brush and color.
- Draw or paint with easy controls.
- Save your drawing or share it directly from the app.
